Best Part

So me and my sister decided to cover a song from our favorite artists Daniel Caesar and H.E.R.“Best Part” is a beautiful song of affection sung by Canadian artist Daniel Caesar and California representative H.E.R. Put succinctly, it’s a love song where the two artists, taking on the roles of lovers, express their appreciation for having each other in their lives.
